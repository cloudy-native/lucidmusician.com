# About Lucid Musician

Lucid Musician is one of the projects of Stephen Harrison (https://dinsor.org). I’ve always been interested in the overlap between music and math, and I spent my career in software development. Now that I have some breathing space, I can lean into music more.

For a long time I wanted to build a music plugin, and LucidHarmony is me finally doing it.

The original itch was pretty simple: I wanted a plugin that could generate ambient chords for a background track. I tried a couple options, but they were either pricey, not quite what I was looking for, or both—so I decided to build one instead.

My background in data science and machine learning should help with the “AI” side of things. On the other hand, DAW plugin frameworks are their own world, so I’m learning those parts as I go.

If you try LucidHarmony, let me know what you think.

After a long career in software development, I’m now retired in the Land of Smiles. Bangkok is my home, and the best part of retirement is getting to work on exactly the things I’m passionate about.

I’m always interested in helping other people with their projects. If you’ve got something you’re building and you’d like an extra brain on it, get in touch with me on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/stephenharrison/). I do most things for fun. If it turns into something a bit more, I'm very open to talk about that too.
